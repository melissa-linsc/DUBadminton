Join Us!

How to Join

Preseason

During the summer, there is the chance to apply to join us for our preseason before the start of the year. It is a fun two weeks filled with training sessions, fitness and lots of socials! Applications are announced on our socials so be sure to check them out!

Trials

We host trial sessions at the beginning of Michaelmas term. Trials are held at Maiden Castle, where captains will select the squad! There is a small fee of £4 to trial to cover costs and you will need your own racquet! If you can't make the trial, we host a retrial for those that missed the first one!

If you are looking to apply throughout the year, all you need to do is contact us and we can arrange to see you play!

Development Squad

For casual play, sign up for our development squad. There are weekly training sessions on Sundays 11-1pm for our development squad, available to all abilities. Sessions are led by our development squad coordinator as well as other DUBADC members, consisting of coaching and games! It is a pay-per-session of £3 or an annual subscription of £40 for the year and your own racquet is required.
